Jollof Rice & Beans Recipe.

Description:

Rice and beans are a classic combination. As the name implies, it’s rice cooked together with beans. It’s inexpensive, nutritious, and goes with just about any meal.

Ingredients:

  • Rice.
  • Beans.
  • Oil.
  • Salt and seasoning.
  • Tomatoe puree.
  • Crayfish.
  • Pepper.
  • Garlic and Ginger.

Steps for preparation:

  1. Place beans in a pot, add 8 cups of water and begin to cook. When beans has cooked for about 30 mins, add half of the chopped onions into the beans, then add bouillon powder to taste.
  2. Begin to cook rice in a separate pot. Cook till its done. Don't cook till it becomes mushy.
  3. Blend bell pepper, tomatoes, habanero pepper and one onion and set aside
  4. In a saucepan heat up oil, stir in the remainder of chopped onions. Stir until softened and translucent.
  5. Pour in the blended pepper mix, cook till reduced in size. Takes about 45 mins. Turn off heat while you wait for the beans to get cooked
  6. When the beans is ready, mix the sauce, rice and beans together.
  7. Add in about a ¼ cup of water stir in, taste for seasoning and adjust accordingly.
  8. Place pot over medium low heat and allow to simmer for about 2 minutes. then stir and turn off heat.
